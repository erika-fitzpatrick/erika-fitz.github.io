ABOUT ME

I am currently finishing up my final semester at the University of Tennessee Knoxville School of Information Science where I am participating in the IMLS-funded CALL program, and I am extremely excited to be beginning my career in librarianship! I hope to merge my interests in community engagement and data-driven program design in order to provide library services that reach underserved populations and are tailored to the specific needs and preferences of the library's entire community. My undergraduate degree is in computer science and I worked as a software developer until my service-oriented mindset and zeal for information literacy led me to make a career change into librarianship, so I would also love to put my technical skillset to use in a library setting, whether by leading coding programming for patrons or helping out with library tech behind the scenes.
